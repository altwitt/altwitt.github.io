# Functional and Versatile map making with Openlayers
As I was watching a course on Openlayers, the convenience, its functionality and versatility really struck me as something to behold. It really gives you an appreciation of open source mapping resources. and display options, and it is on par or better than anything you can do with ArcGIS. Check it out for yourself– Then make a wordburger LOL. But for serial, openlayers has damn near 100 examples including:
